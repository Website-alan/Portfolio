BSCMS

A professional-grade, locally-hosted web application designed to digitize and streamline credit tracking for retail businesses. Replaces paper ledgers with robust digital accountability.

Overview

The **Benny Stores Credit Management System (BSCMS)** is an enterprise-grade financial engine designed to maximize retail liquidity. By digitizing manual khatta ledgers into a high-integrity SQL architecture, it ensures absolute financial accountability.

Python/Django SQLite High-Integrity Malayalam Localization Holographic Glassmorphism Audit Logging Data Core Engine

💰 Financial Audit Log

Every price change and transaction is timestamped and recorded, providing a full transparent history of business actions.

🇮🇳 Regional Support

Specialized character encoding for Malayalam character support, enabling enhanced local usability for store owners.

🏦 Multi-Account Tracking

Manage multiple financial sources simultaneously, from Bank balances (SBI) to physical cash drawers.

📱 Mobile Shop Floor

Optimized for use on tablets and smartphones connected to the shop Wi-Fi, allowing inventory checks on the move.
