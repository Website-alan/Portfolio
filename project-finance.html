Project Overview

A modern, frontend-heavy group finance manager engineered to solve the complexities of shared expenses, trips, and personal wallet management. The application elegantly untangles complex group debts into optimized settlement instructions.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Dashboard: Global view of group expenses, active members, and highly visual key metric statistics.
  • Group Expense Engine: Intelligent capability to split costs across multiple participants and assign distinct situational themes (Scenarios/Trips).
  • Optimized Settlements: A built-in mathematical algorithm that reduces complex group debts into the absolute minimum number of peer-to-peer transactions required to settle up.
  • Personal Wallet Module: A dedicated suite for tracking personal income, private expenses, and net-balance strictly separate from the group ledger.
  • Polished Premium UI: A sleek dark-mode glassmorphic interface loaded with modular sidebar navigation, full responsiveness, and dynamic tabular data handling.
